  Key design decisions (Senior NetDevOps perspective):
  - Inventory is validated before Nornir loads it — bad IPs / unsupported platforms / dangling group refs fail at startup, not mid-run against live gear.
  - Credentials are injected into nr.inventory.defaults at runtime so secrets never land in YAML/git.
  - scrapli asyncssh transport + Nornir threaded runner = concurrent async I/O per device without hand-rolling asyncio.gather().
  - --validate-only mode + deterministic exit codes make it drop-in safe for CI/CD pre-change gates.
